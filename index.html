About Us

Welocme To Foodies Hut Restaturant


At Spice of India, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey through the vibrant and diverse flavors of India. Our story is one of passion for authentic Indian cuisine, and it's our pleasure to bring a taste of India to your table.


Our kitchen is a haven of spices and aromas, where traditional Indian recipes are transformed into mouthwatering dishes that capture the essence of India. Our skilled chefs use a symphony of herbs and spices, along with the freshest ingredients, to craft dishes that reflect the rich tapestry of Indian cuisine.


We invite you to savor the rich tapestry of India's flavors right here at Spice of India. Let us take you on a journey through the bustling markets of Delhi, the coastal kitchens of Kerala, and the royal feasts of Rajasthan. Join us and experience India's culinary diversity like never before. Thank you for choosing foodie hut, and we look forward to sharing a Spice of India with you soon.
